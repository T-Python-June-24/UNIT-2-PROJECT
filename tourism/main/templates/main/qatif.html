The Eastern Coast Gem

Qatif is one of the oldest settlements in Eastern Arabia; its history goes back to 3500 BC, more than 5000 years ago, and was part of the Bahrain Region which was called Dilmun at that time and the Sumerians knew it as the land of paradise, immortality, and life. Qatif Nowadays consists of more than 30 different cities and villages. It also has the second largest island after Bhrain in the area. Qatif has a rich history and a lot of places to visit that tells you about the old days. one of the most Imazing places to visit in Qatif is Tarut Island which has an old castle that goes in history to about 5000 years. It's famous for its local products such as dates, tomato, and lemon. The population of Qatif is about 700K and it has 13 different sports clubs.
