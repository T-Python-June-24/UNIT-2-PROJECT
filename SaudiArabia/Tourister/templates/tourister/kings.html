History of Saudi Arabian Kings

This timeline highlights the reigns of the Saudi Arabian kings from the founding of modern Saudi Arabia to the present day.

King Abdulaziz

Reign: 1932 - 1953

Founder of modern Saudi Arabia, he united the four regions into a single state through a series of conquests.

King Saud

Reign: 1953 - 1964

Son of King Abdulaziz, he focused on developing the country's infrastructure.

King Faisal

Reign: 1964 - 1975

Known for his modernization and reform policies, and significant contributions to foreign affairs.

King Khalid

Reign: 1975 - 1982

Oversaw rapid economic and infrastructure development during his reign.

King Fahd

Reign: 1982 - 2005

Introduced the Basic Law of Governance and was known for his contributions to the modernization of Saudi Arabia.

King Abdullah

Reign: 2005 - 2015

Promoted economic, educational, and social reforms, and expanded women's rights in Saudi Arabia.

King Salman

Reign: 2015 - Present

Current king, known for Vision 2030 initiative aiming to diversify the Saudi economy and reduce its dependence on oil.
